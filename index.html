What is Sneer?

Sneer is an open source platform where you can share anything you want with anyone you care, with no servers in the way, no eavesdropping middlemen, no companies that own your data, no strings attached, no giving up your freedom. It's your stuff, your computer, your friends... your decision. We're giving you the means. It's cool, it's easy, and it's free.
